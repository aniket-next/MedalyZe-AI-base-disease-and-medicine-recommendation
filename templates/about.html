About MedalyZe

What is MedalyZe?

MedalyZe is a beginner-friendly web project designed to help people understand their health better. Users can enter their symptoms and the system will predict the possible disease using artificial intelligence (AI). It also recommends doctors and shows helpful details like BMI (Body Mass Index) calculation.

Why did we build it?

Many people don't know which disease they might have or which doctor to visit. MedalyZe solves this problem using smart technology. Our goal was to make healthcare more accessible and easier to understand for everyone â€” especially for those who may not have medical knowledge.
